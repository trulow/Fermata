# Privacy Policy Last updated: March 5, 2026 Fermata Auto respects your privacy. This application is designed to operate entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit personal information. ## Data Collection Fermata Auto does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The application runs locally on your device and does not send information to external servers. ## Permissions The app may request certain permissions in order to function properly. These permissions are used only to enable core features of the application. Depending on how the app is used, permissions may include: • Access to media files stored on the device to play audio or video • Accessibility access when required for Android Auto integration • Storage access to read local media files These permissions are used strictly for functionality and are not used to collect or transmit personal information. ## Third Party Services Fermata Auto does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking services. ## Data Storage All data used by the application remains on the user's device. No data is uploaded or stored on external servers. ## Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be reflected by the revised date at the top of this document. ## Contact If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue in this repository.
